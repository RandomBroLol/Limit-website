Important Security Tips for Roblox Users

1. Use a Strong Password

Do not use simple passwords like "123456789" or "password". Always create unique, strong passwords to protect your account.

2. Never Share Your Password

Even if someone claims to offer you free Roblox Premium or Robux, never share your password. Roblox admins will never ask for your password.

3. Turn on 2-Step Verification (2SV)

Enable 2-step verification for an extra layer of security. Even if someone gets your password, they won’t be able to access your account without a special verification code sent to your email.

To set up 2SV, go to Settings > Security > Two Step Verification and follow the instructions.

4. Enable Account PIN

Set a 4-digit Account PIN to prevent unauthorized changes to your account settings, such as email or password. If you forget your PIN, you'll need to contact Roblox Support for recovery.

5. Don't Share Your Email Address

Ensure only your email address is linked to your Roblox account. Sharing email addresses could lead to unauthorized access.

6. Avoid Using Your Credentials Outside Roblox

Never use your Roblox username and password on non-Roblox websites or in surveys. This is a common method used by hackers to steal account credentials.
